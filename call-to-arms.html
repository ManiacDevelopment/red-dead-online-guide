Call to Arms Guide

Call to Arms is a survival mode in Red Dead Online where players defend towns against increasingly difficult enemy waves.

Best Weapons

TOP PICK
Lancaster Repeater
Reliable for most combat situations and excellent for headshots.
CLOSE RANGE
Pump Action Shotgun
Extremely effective against rushing enemies.
LONG RANGE
Carcano Rifle
Strong sniper option for eliminating dangerous targets early.

Best Ability Card Build

Recommended Build
  • Paint It Black
  • Iron Lung
  • Strange Medicine
  • Cold Blooded
Strong mix of survivability and damage.

Best Strategies

  • Use rooftops and elevated positions.
  • Focus armored enemies first.
  • Use tonics before difficult waves.
  • Keep weapons clean and fully loaded.
  • Revive teammates whenever possible.
  • Use shotguns when enemies push aggressively.

Recommended Consumables

Tonics
Bring health tonics, Dead Eye tonics and stamina tonics.
Ammo
Express ammo and shotgun shells are highly recommended.
Food
Bring strong core-restoring food before starting.

Common Mistakes

  • Standing in open areas too long.
  • Ignoring armored enemies.
  • Not using tonics early enough.
  • Splitting too far away from teammates.
  • Using weak close-range weapons.
