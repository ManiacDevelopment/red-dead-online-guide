Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to some of the most common questions about Red Dead Online.

What is the best role for beginners?
Bounty Hunter is usually the best first role because it gives gold, money and XP.
What is the fastest way to make money?
Collector is generally the strongest money-making role for solo players.
What is the best weapon?
The Lancaster Repeater is one of the best all-around weapons in the game.
What should I buy first?
Prioritize strong weapons, ability cards and roles before cosmetics.
Is PvP difficult for beginners?
PvP can be challenging early, but good positioning and defensive cards help a lot.
Should I spend gold on cosmetics?
Usually no. Unlock important roles first before buying expensive cosmetics.
What is the best horse?
Many horses are viable. Saddles and stirrups often matter more than the horse itself.
Are daily challenges worth doing?
Yes. Daily challenges are one of the best long-term gold farming methods.
What role is best for passive income?
Trader and Moonshiner are the strongest passive income roles.
Can I play solo?
Absolutely. Collector and Bounty Hunter are excellent for solo players.
