Best Weapons in Red Dead Online

You do not need to buy every weapon. Focus on a few strong weapons that cover the situations you actually play: PvE, hunting, bounties, PvP and long-range combat.

Best Early Weapons

TOP PRIORITY
Lancaster Repeater
One of the best all-around weapons in Red Dead Online. Great for missions, free roam, PvE and general combat.
HUNTING
Varmint Rifle
Essential for small animals and clean hunting. Very useful if you want to make money from pelts.
RIFLE
Bolt Action Rifle
Excellent for hunting, PvE and medium-to-long range combat. One of the best value purchases.

Best Midgame Weapons

CLOSE RANGE
Pump Action Shotgun
Very strong in close-range fights, missions and bounties.
SIDEARM
Schofield Revolver
Reliable revolver with good damage and accuracy.
SNIPER
Rolling Block Rifle
Good early sniper option before you unlock stronger long-range weapons.

Best Late Game Weapons

S-TIER
Carcano Rifle
One of the strongest long-range weapons in the game. Excellent for sniper builds and PvP.
SIDEARM
LeMat Revolver
Strong and flexible revolver with an alternate shotgun shot.
SHOTGUN
Semi-Auto Shotgun
High fire rate and very dangerous at close range.

Recommended Loadouts

  • PvE: Lancaster Repeater, Bolt Action Rifle, Pump Action Shotgun
  • Hunting: Varmint Rifle, Bolt Action Rifle, Bow
  • Bounty Hunter: Lancaster Repeater, Bolt Action Rifle, Pump Action Shotgun
  • Sniper: Carcano Rifle, Lancaster Repeater, strong sidearm
  • PvP: Lancaster Repeater, Carcano Rifle, shotgun or revolvers

Weapons To Avoid Early

  • Expensive weapons you do not actually use
  • Cosmetic weapon variants before core upgrades
  • Too many sidearms early in the game
  • Late-game ammo before you have a stable income
