Gold Farming Guide

Gold is one of the most important currencies in Red Dead Online. You need gold to unlock roles, cosmetics and special content.

Best Gold Farming Methods

BEST FOR BEGINNERS
Daily Challenges
Complete at least one challenge every day to build streak bonuses over time.
TOP METHOD
Bounty Hunter
One of the best and most consistent ways to earn gold through missions.
FREE GOLD
Treasure Maps
Treasure maps reward gold and cash and should always be completed.
BONUS EVENTS
Weekly Bonuses
Rockstar frequently boosts payouts for specific activities.

Best Beginner Strategy

  • Complete at least one daily every day
  • Save gold for your first role
  • Unlock Bounty Hunter first
  • Complete treasure maps immediately
  • Avoid cosmetic purchases early

Fastest Gold Progression

Step 1
Daily challenges and stranger missions.
Step 2
Unlock Bounty Hunter using saved gold.
Step 3
Combine Bounty Hunter with daily challenges.
Step 4
Farm weekly bonus events whenever available.

Common Mistakes

  • Buying expensive cosmetics too early
  • Ignoring daily challenge streaks
  • Not using treasure maps
  • Buying every weapon immediately
  • Ignoring weekly bonuses
