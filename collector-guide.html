Collector Guide

Collector is one of the best roles in Red Dead Online for earning cash and XP. It is especially strong for solo players who enjoy exploration.

Why Collector Is Strong

TOP MONEY ROLE
Massive XP Gains
Collector provides some of the best XP farming in the game.
SOLO FRIENDLY
Easy Solo Gameplay
You can progress efficiently without needing a posse.
HIGH CASH
Strong Cash Rewards
Full collectible sets sell for large amounts of money.

Best Collectibles

  • Coins
  • Jewelry
  • Tarot Cards
  • Heirlooms
  • Fossils

Best Strategy

Step 1
Unlock Collector and purchase the Collector Bag.
Step 2
Focus on easy collectible routes such as Tarot Cards.
Step 3
Save complete sets before selling whenever possible.
Step 4
Combine Collector with daily challenges for extra gold.

Collector Tips

  • Use an online collector map for efficiency.
  • Sell complete sets for maximum profit.
  • Collector is one of the fastest ways to level up.
  • Good horse stamina helps with long routes.
  • Collector combines very well with exploration gameplay.

Common Mistakes

  • Selling collectibles individually too early.
  • Ignoring full set bonuses.
  • Not combining routes efficiently.
  • Skipping easy collectible categories.
  • Forgetting to loot enemies during missions.
