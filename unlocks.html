Level Unlocks

This guide shows important unlocks in Red Dead Online including weapons, equipment, ammo and useful upgrades.

Important Early Unlocks

Level Unlock Why It Matters
8 Fishing Rod Fishing becomes available.
10 Off-Hand Holster Allows dual wielding.
12 Varmint Rifle Important for hunting small animals.
14 Lancaster Repeater One of the best weapons in the game.
21 Bolt Action Rifle Excellent hunting and combat weapon.
22 Pump Action Shotgun Very strong close-range weapon.
50 Carcano Rifle One of the strongest sniper rifles.

Recommended Unlock Priorities

  • Lancaster Repeater
  • Bolt Action Rifle
  • Good saddle and stirrups
  • Important ability cards
  • Role unlocks

What To Avoid Buying Early

  • Expensive cosmetic outfits
  • Weapon skins and variants
  • Too many sidearms
  • Late-game ammo types

Best Early Game Setup

  • Lancaster Repeater
  • Bolt Action Rifle
  • Pump Action Shotgun
  • Paint It Black
  • Bounty Hunter role
