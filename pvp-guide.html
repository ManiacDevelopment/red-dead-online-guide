PvP Guide

PvP in Red Dead Online is heavily influenced by positioning, weapon choice, ability cards and movement.

Best PvP Weapons

META
Lancaster Repeater
Strong accuracy, range and overall reliability.
LONG RANGE
Carcano Rifle
One of the strongest sniper weapons for PvP.
CLOSE RANGE
Pump Action Shotgun
Extremely dangerous at short range.

Best PvP Ability Cards

Paint It Black
Very strong offensive Dead Eye card with fast headshot potential.
Never Without One
Strong defensive card against headshots.
Fool Me Once
Reduces incoming damage over time.
Sharpshooter
Excellent for sniper-focused builds.

Best Beginner PvP Build

  • Paint It Black
  • Iron Lung
  • Fool Me Once
  • Strange Medicine

Balanced setup with survivability and reliable combat power.

PvP Tips

  • Use cover constantly.
  • Do not stand still while shooting.
  • Headshots are extremely important.
  • Keep your weapons clean.
  • Tonics can dramatically increase survivability.
  • Positioning matters more than pure aim in many fights.

Common PvP Mistakes

  • Using weak ability cards.
  • Ignoring Dead Eye usage.
  • Standing in open areas.
  • Using the wrong weapon range.
  • Ignoring defensive cards.
