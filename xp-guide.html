XP Farming Guide

Fast XP progression unlocks weapons, equipment, horses and important upgrades in Red Dead Online. This guide focuses on the fastest and most efficient XP methods.

Best XP Methods

TOP XP METHOD
Collector Routes
One of the fastest XP farming methods in the game. Excellent for solo players.
COMBAT XP
Bounty Hunter
Strong XP gains while also farming gold and money.
BONUS EVENTS
Featured Series
Weekly PvP bonuses can provide huge XP rewards.
SURVIVAL
Call to Arms
Very strong XP if you survive deep into the waves.

Fastest Beginner XP Strategy

Step 1
Complete daily challenges and treasure maps.
Step 2
Unlock Bounty Hunter for combat XP and gold.
Step 3
Unlock Collector for massive XP farming.
Step 4
Combine Collector routes with daily challenges.

Best XP Tips

  • Play during weekly XP bonus events.
  • Use Collector for efficient solo progression.
  • Combine multiple activities together.
  • Loot enemies during combat missions.
  • Upgrade ability cards while leveling.

Common XP Mistakes

  • Grinding low-XP activities for too long.
  • Ignoring Collector progression.
  • Skipping weekly bonuses.
  • Buying expensive cosmetics instead of progression tools.
  • Playing inefficient mission loops repeatedly.
