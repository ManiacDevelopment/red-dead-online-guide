Naturalist Guide

Naturalist is a unique role focused on animals, exploration and legendary creature encounters. It is one of the more relaxed and exploration-heavy roles in Red Dead Online.

What Does Naturalist Do?

ANIMAL STUDY
Collect Animal Samples
Sedate animals, collect samples and complete animal categories.
LEGENDARY ANIMALS
Special Hunts
Unlock legendary animal missions and rare encounters.
EXPLORATION
Relaxed Gameplay
Ideal for players who enjoy exploration and slower-paced gameplay.

Best Naturalist Strategy

Step 1
Buy sedative ammo and begin sampling common animals.
Step 2
Complete animal collections for Naturalist XP.
Step 3
Unlock legendary animal missions.
Step 4
Combine Naturalist with Trader for strong hunting value.

Best Naturalist Tips

  • Carry plenty of sedative ammo.
  • Use tracking vision often.
  • Study animals before sampling them.
  • Legendary animals are valuable for Trader materials.
  • Naturalist works well with exploration-focused gameplay.

Best Activities To Combine

  • Trader hunting
  • Collector exploration routes
  • Daily challenges
  • Legendary animal farming

Common Mistakes

  • Killing animals instead of sampling them.
  • Ignoring legendary animal missions.
  • Forgetting to restock sedative ammo.
  • Not combining the role with Trader.
