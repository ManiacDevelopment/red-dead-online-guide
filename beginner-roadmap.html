Beginner Roadmap

This roadmap helps new players progress efficiently in Red Dead Online without wasting money on bad purchases or unnecessary gear.

Rank 1–10

EARLY GAME
Focus on:
  • Daily challenges
  • Treasure maps
  • Stranger missions
  • Saving gold
Do not waste money on cosmetics early.

Rank 10–20

FIRST UPGRADES
Prioritize:
  • Off-hand holster
  • Lancaster Repeater
  • Varmint Rifle
  • Paint It Black

Rank 20–40

ROLE PROGRESSION
Recommended priorities:
  • Unlock Bounty Hunter first
  • Farm gold and XP
  • Buy Bolt Action Rifle
  • Upgrade ability cards

Rank 40–60

MID GAME
Focus on:
  • Collector role for money
  • Better horse equipment
  • Pump Action Shotgun
  • Passive income roles

Late Game Goals

  • Max level ability cards
  • Efficient gold farming
  • Carcano Rifle
  • Moonshiner and Trader income loops
  • Optimized PvE or PvP build

Most Important Beginner Tips

  • Do not rush cosmetic purchases.
  • Focus on one strong build first.
  • Prioritize roles over expensive weapons.
  • Always complete at least one daily challenge.
  • Treasure maps are extremely valuable early.
