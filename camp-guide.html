Camp Guide

Your camp is one of the most important systems in Red Dead Online. It acts as your base of operations, especially for the Trader role.

Why Camps Matter

BASE OF OPERATIONS
Central Hub
Your camp gives access to fast travel, crafting, cooking and role systems.
TRADER ROLE
Cripps Production
Trader goods are produced directly through your camp.
UTILITY
Useful Upgrades
Camp upgrades can improve convenience and efficiency.

Best Camp Upgrades

Fast Travel Post
One of the best quality-of-life upgrades in the game.
Stew Pot
Provides strong food buffs and core restoration.
Weapon Locker
Helps organize your loadouts by hiding unused weapons.

Best Camp Locations

  • Great Plains
  • Heartlands
  • Big Valley
  • Tall Trees

These locations are popular because they provide strong hunting and easy travel routes.

Camp Tips

  • Keep your Trader production running constantly.
  • Place your camp near strong hunting areas.
  • Use camp cooking for free healing and buffs.
  • Upgrade fast travel early if possible.
  • Use the weapon locker to simplify combat loadouts.

Common Mistakes

  • Ignoring camp upgrades.
  • Leaving Trader production inactive.
  • Choosing poor hunting locations.
  • Not using camp resources regularly.
