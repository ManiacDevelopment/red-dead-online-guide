Daily Challenges Guide

Daily challenges are one of the best ways to earn gold consistently in Red Dead Online. Even casual players should try to complete at least one challenge per day.

Why Daily Challenges Matter

CONSISTENT GOLD
Reliable Income
Daily challenges provide steady gold rewards over time.
STREAK BONUSES
Daily Streak System
Consecutive days increase your gold payouts significantly.
BEGINNER FRIENDLY
Easy To Start
Many challenges can be completed quickly with little effort.

Best Beginner Strategy

Step 1
Log in every day, even if only briefly.
Step 2
Complete at least one simple challenge to maintain your streak.
Step 3
Prioritize quick objectives such as cooking, crafting or harvesting.
Step 4
Combine daily challenges with your active role progression.

Best Challenges To Prioritize

  • Cooking food
  • Collecting herbs
  • Skinning animals
  • Easy role objectives
  • Travel and exploration tasks

Daily Challenge Tips

  • Never break your streak if possible.
  • Quick challenges are usually the most efficient.
  • Combine dailies with Collector or Trader routes.
  • Role challenges become extremely valuable later.
  • Treasure maps and dailies work very well together.

Common Mistakes

  • Ignoring streak bonuses.
  • Trying to complete every challenge every day.
  • Forgetting role-specific dailies.
  • Wasting too much time on difficult objectives.
