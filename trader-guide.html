Trader Guide

Trader is one of the best passive income roles in Red Dead Online. It is built around hunting animals, supplying your camp and selling goods.

Why Trader Is Good

PASSIVE INCOME
Camp Production
Cripps turns animal materials into goods while you do other activities.
HUNTING
Perfect For Hunters
Trader rewards players who enjoy hunting and exploring the wilderness.
POSSE FRIENDLY
Great With Friends
Deliveries are easier and safer when playing with a posse.

Best Trader Strategy

Step 1
Hunt high-quality animals and donate them to Cripps.
Step 2
Keep materials and supplies full so production never stops.
Step 3
Do bounties, collectibles or dailies while goods are being produced.
Step 4
Sell goods when your delivery wagon is ready.

Local vs Long Distance Delivery

SAFER
Local Delivery
Lower payout, but safer and faster. Recommended if you are solo.
HIGHER PAYOUT
Long Distance Delivery
Better payout, but higher risk. Best with a posse.

Best Animals For Trader

  • Perfect deer carcasses
  • Perfect buck carcasses
  • Perfect cougars
  • Perfect panthers
  • Legendary animal carcasses

Trader Tips

  • Always aim for 3-star animals.
  • Use the correct weapon to preserve pelt quality.
  • Move camp near good hunting areas.
  • Do other activities while Cripps produces goods.
  • Use local deliveries if you are playing alone.
