Red Dead Online Tier Lists

Quick overview of the strongest and most effective choices in Red Dead Online. Tier lists are guidelines — your playstyle and budget still matter.

S-Tier

  • Lancaster Repeater — Best all-around repeater.
  • Bolt Action Rifle — Excellent for hunting and PvE.
  • Paint It Black — One of the strongest ability cards.
  • Collector — Best solo cash and XP role.

A-Tier

  • Pump Action Shotgun — Powerful close-range weapon.
  • Bounty Hunter — Great for gold and combat.
  • Iron Lung — Strong defensive ability card.
  • Moonshiner — Reliable passive income.

B-Tier

  • Rolling Block Rifle — Strong but more situational.
  • Naturalist — Fun role with legendary animals.
  • Sharpshooter — Good for sniper builds.

C-Tier

  • Machete — Mostly for fun and style.
  • Double-Action Revolver — Very niche weapon.
  • Expensive cosmetic horses — Low priority early game.

How To Use Tier Lists

  • S-tier items are usually worth prioritizing.
  • A-tier items are strong alternatives.
  • B-tier items are situational but still useful.
  • C-tier items are mostly optional or style-based.

Remember: the best setup is the one that matches your playstyle.
