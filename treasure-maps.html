Treasure Maps Guide

Treasure maps are one of the easiest and most valuable sources of gold and cash in Red Dead Online, especially for newer players.

Why Treasure Maps Matter

FREE GOLD
Gold Rewards
Treasure chests usually contain gold, cash and useful supplies.
BEGINNER FRIENDLY
Easy Early Progression
Treasure maps are extremely useful for unlocking your first role.
LOW RISK
Safe Rewards
You can complete treasure maps without difficult combat.

How To Get Treasure Maps

  • Level rewards every few ranks
  • Looting enemies
  • Random encounters
  • Treasure hunters in free roam
  • Battle pass and event rewards

Best Strategy

Step 1
Open your satchel and activate the treasure map.
Step 2
Travel to the highlighted search area.
Step 3
Search carefully using Eagle Eye and visual clues.
Step 4
Open the chest and collect your rewards.

Treasure Map Tips

  • Always complete treasure maps quickly.
  • Treasure maps are excellent for early gold.
  • Use fast travel to speed up routes.
  • Treasure maps combine well with exploration gameplay.
  • Loot enemies during missions for extra map chances.

Common Mistakes

  • Ignoring treasure maps for too long.
  • Forgetting to loot enemies.
  • Missing random treasure hunter encounters.
  • Not using Eagle Eye while searching.
