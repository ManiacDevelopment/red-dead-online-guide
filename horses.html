Best Horses in Red Dead Online

Horses are an important part of Red Dead Online, but expensive horses are not always necessary. Saddles and stirrups often matter more than the horse itself.

Best Early Game Horses

BEGINNER
Kentucky Saddler
Cheap and reliable early horse while saving money for better upgrades.
GOOD VALUE
American Standardbred
Fast enough for most early-game activities.

Best Midgame Horses

POPULAR
Missouri Fox Trotter
One of the most popular horses thanks to strong speed and handling.
BALANCED
Turkoman
Excellent balance between speed, stamina and toughness.

Best Late Game Horses

TOP TIER
Arabian
Very fast and responsive horse with elite handling.
TOP TIER
Norfolk Roadster
Excellent horse for Moonshiner players and fast travel.

Best Saddle Setup

  • Nacogdoches Saddle
  • Best available stirrups
  • Prioritize stamina drain reduction

Horse Tips

  • Do not spend all your money on horses early.
  • Saddles matter more than many horse upgrades.
  • Most horses are viable for normal gameplay.
  • Choose horses you actually enjoy riding.
  • Higher bonding levels improve horse performance.
