About This Website

Red Dead Online Guide is a fan-made website created to help both new and experienced players progress more efficiently in Red Dead Redemption 2 Online.

What This Website Covers

  • Best weapons and loadouts
  • Money and gold farming
  • Role progression guides
  • Ability card builds
  • Beginner progression roadmaps
  • PvP and PvE strategies
  • Daily challenges and weekly bonuses
  • Interactive trackers and calculators

Why This Site Exists

Many new players waste money and gold on weak equipment or inefficient progression paths. This guide is designed to help players avoid common mistakes and enjoy the game more.

The goal is to create one central place for:

  • Efficient progression
  • Useful beginner advice
  • Strong builds
  • Role recommendations
  • Easy-to-understand guides

Planned Features

  • Interactive collectible maps
  • Weapon comparison tools
  • More advanced build generators
  • Role profit calculators
  • Community tier lists
  • News and event tracking
