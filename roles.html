Roles Guide

Roles are the main progression system in Red Dead Online. They unlock new activities, income methods, cosmetics, gear and gameplay loops.

Best First Role

BEST FOR GOLD
Bounty Hunter
Bounty Hunter is often the best first role because it allows you to earn gold, cash and XP while playing combat-focused missions.
BEST SOLO CASH
Collector
Collector is the best role for solo cash and XP. It is perfect if you enjoy exploring the map and collecting sets.

All Roles Explained

GOLD
Bounty Hunter
Hunt targets, complete legendary bounties and earn gold. Great for combat-focused players.
CASH & XP
Collector
Find collectibles across the map and sell complete sets for strong money and XP.
PASSIVE CASH
Trader
Hunt animals, supply your camp and deliver goods for profit.
PASSIVE BUSINESS
Moonshiner
Produce moonshine and sell batches for reliable income.
ANIMALS
Naturalist
Track, study and sample animals. Also gives access to legendary animals.

Recommended Role Order

  • 1. Bounty Hunter — if you need gold.
  • 2. Collector — if you want cash and XP.
  • 3. Trader — if you enjoy hunting and passive income.
  • 4. Moonshiner — if you want another passive business.
  • 5. Naturalist — best for variety and legendary animals.

Which Role Should You Pick?

  • Want gold? Pick Bounty Hunter.
  • Want solo cash? Pick Collector.
  • Like hunting? Pick Trader.
  • Want passive income? Pick Moonshiner.
  • Like animals and exploration? Pick Naturalist.
