Ability cards are one of the most important systems in Red Dead Online. A strong build can completely change your survivability, damage and playstyle.

Ability Card Tips

  • Do not buy too many cards early.
  • Focus on leveling one build first.
  • Paint It Black is one of the safest early investments.
  • Defensive cards are extremely valuable in PvE.
  • Sniper builds are strongest with good aim and positioning.
