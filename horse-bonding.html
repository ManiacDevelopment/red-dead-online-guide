Horse Bonding Guide

Horse bonding improves handling, stamina and overall horse performance.

How To Increase Bonding

  • Ride your horse regularly
  • Feed and brush your horse
  • Calm the horse during rides
  • Avoid unnecessary damage

Bonding Levels

  • Level 1 — Basic
  • Level 2 — Better control
  • Level 3 — Improved stamina
  • Level 4 — Maximum bonding bonuses
