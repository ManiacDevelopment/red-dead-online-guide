Moonshiner Guide

Moonshiner is a strong passive income role in Red Dead Online. It works best when combined with other activities while your moonshine is being produced.

Why Moonshiner Is Good

PASSIVE CASH
Production Runs In The Background
Start a batch, do other activities, then return when it is ready to sell.
SHORT DELIVERIES
Fast Sales
Moonshine deliveries are usually shorter than Trader deliveries.
STORY CONTENT
Moonshiner Story Missions
The role includes its own story missions and unique gameplay.

Best Moonshiner Strategy

Step 1
Start moonshine production as soon as you begin playing.
Step 2
Complete bootlegger missions to reduce mash cost.
Step 3
Do bounties, collectibles or daily challenges while production runs.
Step 4
Return to your shack and sell the batch when it is ready.

Best Activities To Combine With Moonshiner

  • Collector routes
  • Bounty Hunter missions
  • Daily challenges
  • Trader hunting
  • Treasure maps

Moonshiner Tips

  • Do bootlegger missions before starting expensive batches.
  • Drive carefully during deliveries to avoid bottle damage.
  • Use Moonshiner as passive income, not your only activity.
  • Upgrade your operation when you can afford it.
  • Combine Moonshiner with Collector for strong cash flow.

Common Mistakes

  • Ignoring mash cost.
  • Driving too fast and breaking bottles.
  • Waiting idle instead of doing other activities.
  • Buying upgrades before your economy is stable.
