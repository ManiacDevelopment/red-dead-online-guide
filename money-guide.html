Money & Gold Guide

Learn the best methods for earning cash, gold and XP in Red Dead Online. Different methods work better depending on your rank, role and playstyle.

Best Beginner Money Methods

BEST STARTER METHOD
Daily Challenges
Complete at least one easy daily every day. This is one of the safest and easiest ways to build gold early.
Treasure Maps
Treasure maps give gold and cash rewards. Always complete them as soon as possible.
Stranger Missions
Good early-game option for money and XP.

Best Roles For Money

S-TIER
Collector
Best solo money and XP role in the game. Very strong if you enjoy exploring the map.
S-TIER
Bounty Hunter
Best role for gold farming. Great for combat-focused players.
A-TIER
Trader
Excellent passive money role if you enjoy hunting.
A-TIER
Moonshiner
Strong passive income with shorter deliveries.

Best XP Methods

  • Collector runs
  • Featured Series bonuses
  • Legendary Bounties
  • Trader deliveries
  • Call to Arms
  • Daily challenges

Best Gold Farming Methods

  • Daily challenges
  • Bounty Hunter missions
  • Treasure maps
  • Award resets
  • Weekly bonus events

Money Tips

  • Do not waste money on cosmetic items early.
  • Prioritize weapons and roles first.
  • Buy ability cards slowly and level them over time.
  • Always check weekly discounts before buying gear.
  • Combine passive roles with active farming.
