Hunting Guide

Hunting is one of the best ways to support the Trader role and earn money in Red Dead Online. Good hunting routes can provide strong passive income and valuable materials.

Best Hunting Weapons

SMALL ANIMALS
Varmint Rifle
Essential for rabbits, birds and other small animals.
MEDIUM & LARGE
Bolt Action Rifle
One of the best all-around hunting weapons.
STEALTH
Bow
Useful for stealth kills and preserving pelt quality.

Best Hunting Areas

  • Great Plains
  • Big Valley
  • Heartlands
  • Tall Trees
  • Bayou Nwa

Best Animals For Trader

Perfect Deer
Easy to hunt and excellent for Trader materials.
Perfect Buck
Valuable and commonly available.
Cougars & Panthers
Very high material value but dangerous.
Legendary Animals
Extremely valuable for Trader production.

Hunting Tips

  • Always aim for 3-star animals.
  • Use the correct weapon type for clean kills.
  • Headshots help preserve pelt quality.
  • Use Eagle Eye to track animals.
  • Combine hunting with Trader production.

Common Hunting Mistakes

  • Using the wrong weapon for the animal size.
  • Damaging pelts with poor shots.
  • Ignoring legendary animals.
  • Hunting low-value animals too often.
