Activities in Red Dead Online

There is much more to do in Red Dead Online than just grinding money. Different activities reward different things such as gold, XP, cash, cosmetics and fun gameplay.

Roles

GOLD & COMBAT
Bounty Hunter
Hunt targets, complete legendary bounties and earn gold.
BEST SOLO MONEY
Collector
Search the map for collectibles and complete valuable sets.
PASSIVE INCOME
Trader
Hunt animals and sell deliveries from your camp business.
PASSIVE MONEY
Moonshiner
Produce and deliver moonshine for reliable cash.
LEGENDARY ANIMALS
Naturalist
Study and track animals across the map.

Combat Activities

Call to Arms
Survival-style mode where you defend towns against waves of enemies.
Featured Series
PvP playlists with bonus rewards and rotating game modes.
Free Roam Events
Random competitive activities that appear while exploring.

Exploration Activities

Treasure Maps
Search for hidden treasure chests with gold and money rewards.
Hunting
Hunt animals for pelts, trader materials and crafting.
Fishing
Relaxing activity that can also provide decent income.
Collectibles
Search the world for tarot cards, coins, jewelry and fossils.

Best Activities For New Players

  • Daily challenges
  • Bounty Hunter missions
  • Treasure maps
  • Collector routes
  • Trader deliveries with friends

Best Activities For Fun

  • Call to Arms
  • Legendary Bounties
  • Free roam PvP
  • Roleplay and exploration
  • Posse events with friends
