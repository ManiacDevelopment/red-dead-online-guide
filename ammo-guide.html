Ammo Guide

Different ammo types greatly affect weapon performance in Red Dead Online.

Best Ammo Types

  • Express Ammo — Higher damage
  • High Velocity Ammo — Better penetration
  • Split Point Ammo — Improved accuracy
  • Shotgun Slugs — Strong close-range damage

Tips

  • Use Express Ammo for PvE and PvP
  • Keep ammo stocked before missions
  • Special ammo is expensive early
